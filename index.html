Intro

Hi, my name is Ryan Hatrick. I'm an undergraduate student at Sacred Heart University, currently studying Information Technology with a minor in Computer Science. My specialization deals mostly in Computer Hardware and Software.

I have always tinkered with things from a young age, from LEGOs to various electronics. I've been enthralled with the idea of understanding the inner workings of things for a long time, and this led me to build my first computer when I was 13 years old. From working on cars with my father, to building sheds in Construction during high school, I love to keep my mind and hands at work.

Work

I've had many job experiences, ranging from Catering to Robotics. The photo above is from my IT internship at Amity Middle School Orange, I worked with an IT Specialist as my mentor. At this job, I would respond to calls from teachers if they had computer or audio/visual problems, as well as repair laptops for the students. My time there resulted in a greater understanding of networking, audio/visual equipment, and programming langauges.

I have worked the longest with Follett, at the Sacred Heart University Bookstore. Through this, I've helped coworkers with computer issues, as well as learn a great deal about customer service. However, I'm always looking to learn more about technology, whether it's networking, programming, or new gadgets.
